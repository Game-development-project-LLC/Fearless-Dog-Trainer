# Fearless Dog Trainer

**An emotional dog-training simulation about overcoming fear, one step at a time.**  
You play as a young man who was bitten by a dog as a child and now turns his trauma into strength through empathy and training.

 **Goal:** Build trust with different dogs and open your own rehabilitation training center.  
 **Core Features:** Fear/Trust meters, simple keyboard control, emotional training, and gradual learning.  
 **Target Audience:** Kids (ages 10–16) and young adults interested in dogs and emotional growth.

##  About the Game
In **Fearless Dog Trainer**, every small success matters — each treat given, command spoken, or calm step taken reduces fear and increases trust.  
The player learns that true strength comes from patience, empathy, and consistency.
